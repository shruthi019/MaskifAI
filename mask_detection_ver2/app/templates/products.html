Maintain Social Distancing Please stay 6 feet apart

Maintain social distancing when you interact with people.In case of a meeting, make sure there are not too many people in the room at a time so that you can maintain social distancing.

SANITIZE FREQUENTLY Sanitize your workplace and hands

Before beginning your day’s work, disinfect the desk and the laptop/desktop.If you absolutely have to touch a handle or a door knob, sanitise your hands immediately.At lunchtime, wash your hands properly before you start eating.

Avoid Handshake Opt for other alternatives

Handshakes are potential harbingers of covid-19.An infected person would have to sneeze or cough into his hand, touch someone else’s hand, and that second person would then have to place that hand in close contact with their nose, mouth or even eyes to get infected with any virus that might still be on the hand’s surface
